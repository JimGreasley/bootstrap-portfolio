About Me

I was born and raised in a small town in western Canada. After graduating college with a bachelor's degree in computer science, I worked for the provincial telephone company. Eventually I found my way to Los Angeles as part of a team of contractors helping an electronics distributor migrate their business applications to an IBM mainframe. Finding the work challenging and gratifying, I remained with this company and relocated to Phoenix after they moved their corporate headquarters there.

Now, as a recent coding boot camp graduate, I look forward to leveraging my previous career experience in mainframe application development, while embarking on a journey of continuous learning as a full stack web developer. I'm a conscientious worker with a passion for programming and creating elegant software solutions.

I'm proficient in HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JSON, jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, MongoDB, React and Visual Studio Code.

I live in Chandler, AZ with my wife Anne, daughter Stephanie and two cats, Galileo and Einstein. I enjoy reading about astronomy and space exploration and listening to music: from the Beatles to jazz and classical music especially from the baroque period.

Progressive Budget

A progressive web application (PWA) for an online/offline budget tracker. The user will be able to add expenses and deposits to their budget with or without a network connection. Transactions that are entered while offline are saved in IndexedDB. Those transactions will be loaded into the primary MongoDB when online access is re-established.

Technologies used: JavaScript, Express and Express.Router, IndexedDB, MongoDB and Mongoose.
